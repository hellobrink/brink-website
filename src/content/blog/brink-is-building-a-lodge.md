Together with our friends at [UMVA Experiences](https://www.umvamuhazi.com/) we are building an innovation lodge in Rwanda, by Lake Muhazi.

Rwanda is a country that’s already a beacon for innovation, forward thinking, and jaw-dropping natural beauty. It’s the spark behind everything we’re building. UMVA and Brink are teaming up to create a launchpad for ideas, action, and connection. A place where people will one day say, “This is where it all began.” To do this we’re making a space engineered for innovation, with a program of events to spark connection with other dreamers and doers, corners for quiet contemplation and dedicated areas for you to make things happen with your team.

Because it’s for all of us, we’re inviting you to get involved before we open our doors.

## Help us name the lodge

We’re looking for a name for this space that embodies the spirit of Africa, innovation and the promise of a bright future.

If we choose your name, not only will you forever be a part of our story, but we’ll give you a free night at UMVA Muhazi - the adventure lodge that started it all.

Add your name in the comments on [this LinkedIn post](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7282788264179073026/?actorCompanyId=27026293) or email us on [coffee@hellobrink.co](mailto:coffee@hellobrink.co?subject=Lodge%20name%20competition). A winning name will be chosen and shared on the 17th January. (Full Ts & Cs [are right here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ElQ9apx7e485jAUNC2ZbI3WSmYWIKIZ-Sb7LGJEFOaI/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.cnbjpqwnccv9)).

## Partner with us

We’re working with friends to plan events and find exciting ways to use the farm, the buildings and the beauty of the land as a living lab for innovation. If you have ideas (or questions), then [let’s talk!](mailto:coffee@hellobrink.co?subject=I%20want%20to%20get%20involved!)
