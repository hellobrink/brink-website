We are so excited to announce that we're a winner of **The Escape 100**: an annual list of the top purpose-led organisations to work at in 2023, by [Escape the City](https://www.linkedin.com/company/escape-the-city/?ref=hellobrink.co) and we're thrilled to have made it into the top 50 this year, ranking at #43! We're proud to have now been recognised by Escape The City for a [second year](https://hellobrink.co/brinks-in-the-esc-top-100/).

![](/images/66d9d1ab670039e256e63df6_img_0795-1.png)

This year had over 13,000 other company nominations (😱), and we're grateful to be recognised in this way - ranking alongside social enterprise giants like [Too Good To Go](https://www.linkedin.com/company/too-good-to-go/?ref=hellobrink.co), [Origin Coffee](https://www.linkedin.com/company/origin-coffee-roasters/?ref=hellobrink.co), [Rude Health](https://www.linkedin.com/company/rude-health/?ref=hellobrink.co), [Here We Flo](https://www.linkedin.com/company/hereweflo/?ref=hellobrink.co) and [Finisterre](https://www.linkedin.com/company/finisterre/?ref=hellobrink.co).

The [#Escape100](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/?keywords=escape100&highlightedUpdateUrns=urn%3Ali%3Aactivity%3A7084853289866948608&ref=hellobrink.co) campaign was started by Escape the City, and to make the top 100 we were marked against a rigorous scoring system of 6 criteria (Impact, People, Mission, Planet, Innovation and B-Corp) including anonymous reviews from the team, then ranked against the many (many!) other applicants.

🚀 Check us out at [Escape The City](https://bit.ly/3NMRDqM?ref=hellobrink.co)!
