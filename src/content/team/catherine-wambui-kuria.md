Catherine is a mission-driven program manager with over seven years ofexperience working across Africa’s education, health, and entrepreneurship ecosystems.

At Brink, she leads the delivery of high-impact initiatives such as the [Hanga, Sexual & Reproductive Health (SRH)](https://www.hangapitchfest.rw/) program, which supports Africa-based innovators with tech-enabled solutions to refine their ideas, scale their impact, and build long-term sustainability. She also supports the delivery of [uBoraBora](https://www.uborabora.com/), an implementation research fund focused on foundational literacy and numeracy across Sub-Saharan Africa.

She brings deep expertise in fund, program, and grant management, as well as in evidence generation, innovation design, and building strategic partnerships. She holds an MSc in Global Challenges from the University of Edinburgh and is currently pursuing an MSc in Digital Transformation from Nexford University.

Catherine is passionate about democratising access to funding and innovation by designing and delivering programs that support the people, ventures, and ideas shaping a more inclusive and equitable Africa, especially in areas such as health, education, and gender.
