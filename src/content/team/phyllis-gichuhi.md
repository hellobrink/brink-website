Phyllis is an Innovation Manager at Brink working across two programmes. On the [Frontier Tech Hub](https://www.frontiertechhub.org/), she supports FCDO Advisors to test frontier-technology solutions to complex development challenges, helping them design experiments, gather evidence, and translate early-stage pilots into credible pathways for scale. She has worked with Advisors and partners across Africa, Asia and South America.On the [Hanga SRH programme](https://rwanda.unfpa.org/en/news/unfpa-and-ministry-ict-join-forces-harness-technology-gender-equality-and-reproductive-health), she supports Sub-Saharan African ventures using technology to address sexual and reproductive health challenges. Her work focuses on helping founders strengthen their business models ready for scale.

Alongside this, Phyllis works with Brink’s Growth Team to identify and shape new opportunities. She helps align Brink’s stripes and offerings with emerging opportunities across funders and collaborators, while strengthening internal processes to support effective business development.

Before joining Brink, Phyllis worked with [Mr. Green Africa](https://www.mrgreenafrica.com/), Kenya’s largest plastic recycler, helping design circular supply chains that challenge how waste is valued. She developed B2C plastic collection models that strengthened supply and increased the company’s visibility. She also helped establish an early corporate partnership with TotalEnergies to support the rollout of Nairobi’s first plastic buy-back centres.
