Hamza is an Innovation Executive, his role involves supporting the delivery of two programs at Brink: the Hanga Sexual Reproductive Health and the EdTech Funders CoLab. For Hanga, his responsibilities include supporting the program’s team to use agile practices, schedule and coordinate activities, and working on the cohort management. For the EdTech Funders CoLab, his role involves working closely with the program lead to deliver a paper, coordinate the team, deliver virtual events, schedule activities, and stay on budget.
