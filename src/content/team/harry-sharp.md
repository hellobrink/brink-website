UK

Harry is an Innovation Manager at Brink, working across a two of our programmes: Oxygen CoLab and Frontier Technologies Livestreaming. He coaches teams around the world to test and grow their ideas, ventures, and interventions to some of the world’s biggest challenges.

Before joining Brink, Harry spent 10 years working in the London Market Insurance Industry. Most recently, he worked as a Product Manager at LLoyd’s on their Future at Lloyd’s programme – an ambitious strategy to create the world’s most advanced insurance marketplace. Prior to that, he managed a portfolio of complex commercial property claims across the globe. Harry holds an BA(Hons) in English Language from the University of Manchester.

In 2015, Harry co-founded a mobile app development venture and launched two mobile apps for creating live music by recording and layering vocal and instrumental audio loops. He loves visual design and has created logos, websites, and other artwork as a freelancer.
