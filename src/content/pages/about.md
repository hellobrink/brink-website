## Behavioural Innovation: Our proven approach

Behavioural Innovation combines behavioural sciences with innovation methods to deliver lasting impact.

By integrating insights from psychology, anthropology, sociology, and economics, we create the mindsets and mechanisms that deliver real change.

We believe that true innovation happens when we:

- Put people at the centre because the real world has real people in it
- Use insights into how people think and act to fuel breakthrough ideas
- Deeply understand incentives to shift systems
- Understand that transforming mindset is as important as developing skillset

Brink’s mission is to make Behavioural Innovation available to the world’s programmes and funds, innovators and changemakers.

## The minds behind Brink

Our diverse team of experts brings together a wealth of knowledge and experience in behavioural science, innovation, and technology.

From London to Kigali, Barcelona to Nairobi, our passionate thinkers and doers collaborate across borders, tackling complex challenges and drive meaningful change worldwide.

Diversity is in our DNA. It’s more than just the makeup of the team. It also means being able to work in multidisciplinary ways, and to articulate a more global perspective on innovation, which feels representative of the work we do and the innovators we work with. And it means being*where the work is*, and being *of* the places we work, not just parachuting into them.
