principles:
  - title: "Enabling timely action with evidence"
    body:
      - "Health systems often wait for perfect evidence before adopting innovations; delaying action, increasing costs, and missing opportunities to save lives now."
      - "We bring ecosystem stakeholders together to share insights, design experiments to test and learn in areas that haven’t been explored, and iterate on solutions together at speed. As a result, we eliminate the need to reinvent the wheel and enable faster, more effective action."
  - title: "Building competitive healthcare markets"
    body:
      - "Healthcare funding is fragmented by multiple actors and a lack of effective coordination. This weakens buying power and hinders the development of competitive, efficient markets that can effectively address preventable health issues."
      - "We bring ecosystem stakeholders together to align on priorities and leverage collective funds, designing funding models that strengthen supply chains and incentivise demand, to create a thriving market where solutions can scale."
  - title: "Unlocking health tech for the marginalised"
    body:
      - "Innovative health technologies often fail to reach the most marginalised due to regulatory, procurement and distribution barriers. As a result, smaller players struggle to scale, leaving outdated systems in place that overlook vulnerable communities."
      - "We drive local market development by unblocking barriers to growth and, as a result, create solutions that are locally relevant, scalable, and deliver lasting impact."
